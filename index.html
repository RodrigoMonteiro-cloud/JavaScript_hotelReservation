Luxurious Rooms

Stay in a well-appointed room at Radisson with your own fireplace, king-size bed, balcony overlooking the red rocks, private bath with shower, and whirlpool tub for two. Choose from the Canyon, Javelina, Manzanita, Thunder Mountain, and Schnebly rooms — each is unique and sure to be a high point of your vacation experience.

Hearty Breakfast

Each day begins with a hearty breakfast that includes your choice of belgian waffles, pumpkin french toast, or quiche. Fresh fruit, yogurt, juice, and coffee are always available.

About Your Hosts

Your hosts, Melanie and Greg Tortuga, are long-time Radisson residents who love to share their knowledge of the Radisson area with their guests. Melanie is an author and artist. Greg has years of experience leading jeep tours and guiding hikes in and around Sedona.

Guided Hikes

Radisson offers a free guided hike each week for guests. Explore the Seven Sacred Pools along the Soldiers Pass Trail.
